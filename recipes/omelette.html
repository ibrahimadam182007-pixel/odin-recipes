Omelette

Description

Omelette is a scrambled egg that folded and it have creamy texture on the inside

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and seasoning.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add the egg mixture.
  3. Cook for a few minutes until the edges are set.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top and let it melt.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and serve.
