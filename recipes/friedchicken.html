Fried Chicken

Description

A beloved dish featuring chicken pieces coated in seasoned flour or batter and cooked via methods like pan frying, deep-frying, or pressure-frying

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Buttermilk or 1 cup regular milk
  • All purpose flour
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Dry the chicken
  2. Submerge the chicken in buttermilk
  3. Let it sit for 20 minutes or refrigerate overnight
  4. Whisk your flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder
  5. Lift each chicken pieces from the buttermilk, letting the excess drip off
  6. Roll the chicken in the flour mixture, pressing firmly so it sticks completely
  7. Pour vegetable oil into a heavy skillet or deep pot until it is about 1 inch deep
  8. Heat over medium-high heat until the oil reaches 350°F (175°C)
  9. Place pieces skin-side down into the hot oil without overcrowding
  10. Fry dark meat 10-12 minutes
  11. turn the pieces over halfway through to ensure even browning
  12. Ensure the thickest part of the meat reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C)
  13. Place the cooked chicken on a wire cooling rack over a baking sheet to stay crispy
  14. Let it cool for 5 minutes before serving so the juices redistribute
